# Authoring Quality Gate

Owner: Med Auto Grant.

Authoring quality requires AI-authored critique, review closure evidence, quality closure dossier refs, or owner receipt evidence. Scorecards and quality-diff reports organize evidence; they do not independently authorize readiness.

Allowed returns: quality verdict refs, typed blockers, or MAG owner receipt refs.

Forbidden outputs: AI-free quality verdicts, schema-completeness ready verdicts, generic lifecycle completion verdicts, or grant artifact mutation without MAG authority.
