# Specific Aims And Structure Policy

## Entry Conditions

- Fundability handoff exists with proceed/repair state, reviewer risks, and call-fit rationale.
- The funding call, applicant constraints, and route truth are stable enough to shape aims.
- Any return-to-fundability issue is marked before authoring starts.

## Stage Work

- Build central claim, specific aims, innovation/impact logic, methods route, evidence needs, and section map.
- Preserve accepted strategy and funder-specific review criteria.
- Identify unsupported claims and route conflicts before proposal drafting.

## Exit Conditions

- `specific_aims_structure_accepted` exists with aims/structure refs; or
- A quality-debt diagnostic and route-back recommendation records missing fundability handoff, route conflict, unsupported core claim, incomplete section map, or unmitigated review risk; or
- A MAG owner receipt signs aims acceptance.

## Handoff

- Handoff target: `proposal_authoring`.
- Handoff must include accepted aims, section map, claim/evidence ledger, reviewer-risk mitigations, and unresolved source gaps.
- Handoff must name any issue that must return to fundability rather than moving forward.

## Independent Review And Gate Expectation

- Independent review should verify that each aim can win review under the locked call and applicant constraints.
- Required gates: `fundability` and `quality`.
- Aims are not accepted by transition success, provider completion, or descriptor validity.

## OPL Role Boundary

- OPL role: hosted attempt carrier and descriptor consumer.
- OPL cannot replace MAG aims acceptance with generic transition success.
- Allowed action refs: `open_grant_user_loop`, `inspect_progress`.

## Non-Pass Signals

- Aims read like generic objectives instead of a funder-facing review argument.
- Core claims cannot be traced to applicant evidence or accepted fundability strategy.
