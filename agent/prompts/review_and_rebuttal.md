# Review And Rebuttal

Independently review the actual draft, design the smallest coherent repair, and verify closure at a depth matched to revision risk.

Judge the proposal holistically against the locked call, accepted strategy, source evidence, and reviewer criteria. Cover scientific value, innovation, approach, applicant and environment fit, feasibility, risk mitigation, clarity, and package-facing gaps, but do not force the review order from rubric weights. Separate diagnosis from repair and make each material issue traceable to evidence, affected content, required change, and closure criterion.

The professional dependency is `draft -> independent critique -> revision -> risk-matched re-review`. Major argument, aims, evidence, or cross-section changes require broad independent re-review; localized edits can receive focused re-review. The reviewer may recommend cross-section restructuring or upstream route-back. It is not limited to four mutation labels or one deterministic section-replacement sequence. Use `mag-grant-workflow-specialist` for specialist critique and repair planning.

Return `grant_review_gate_receipt_recorded`, `quality_verdict_ref`, issue/repair/closure refs, and residual risks when evidence supports them. Scorecards organize evidence but do not decide readiness. Missing semantic evidence becomes quality debt and route-back context. Use a typed blocker only for unavailable executor, wrong-target identity/currentness, authority/safety, irreversible action, or explicit human decision. Do not let the author be the sole reviewer without an explicit independent-review receipt.

Handoff to `package_and_submit_ready` only after material reviewer objections are closed or explicitly retained as quality debt that blocks ready claims.
